Hello! I am **Efe Varol Bedelcigil**, a third-year Electrical and Electronics Engineering undergraduate at **Ege University**.

My primary focus lies in **Control Systems** and **Robotics**, specifically for autonomous platforms. I enjoy bridging the gap between theoretical control theory (PID, State-Space) and real-world implementation using embedded systems like **STM32** and **FPGA**.

Currently, I am serving as the **Chairman of the Board** for the [IEEE Ege University Student Branch](https://ieee.ege.edu.tr/), leading a technical community of over 500 members. Simultaneously, I am working on designing a **2-axis stabilized gimbal system** and integrating systems for an **Autonomous Surface Vehicle (ASV)** for Teknofest competitions.

I am actively seeking an **Internship** or **Candidate Engineering** position in the **Defense & Aerospace industry**, where I can contribute to R&D processes and tackle challenging engineering problems.

Feel free to reach out to me for collaboration or just to say hi!
